# Brainstorming and Ideation

This file serves as a space for brainstorming and exploring new ideas, potential features, and future directions for the project.

**Purpose:**
To capture creative thoughts and suggestions without immediately committing them to the core vision or active development plans. Ideas documented here can be later reviewed, refined, and potentially integrated into the project through the formal planning process.

**Guidance for Content:**
*   Capture raw ideas, hypotheses, and potential solutions.
*   Briefly explain the rationale or potential benefit of each idea.
*   Ideas documented here do not override the protected `core_vision.md` or `constraints.md` unless they are formally adopted and integrated into those documents.
